Physical Chemistry Formula Sheet

1. Thermodynamics

  • ΔU = q - w (First Law of Thermodynamics)
  • w = -nRT ln(Vf/Vi) (Work done by gas)
  • ΔH = ΔU + PΔV (Enthalpy change)
  • ΔG = ΔH - TΔS (Gibbs free energy)
  • ΔG = -RT ln K (Relation with equilibrium)
  • Cv = (∂U/∂T), Cp = (∂H/∂T) (Heat capacities)

2. Chemical Kinetics

  • r = k[A]^m[B]^n (Rate law)
  • k = Ae^(-Ea/RT) (Arrhenius equation)
  • t₁/₂ = 0.693/k (Half-life)
  • ln[A] = ln[A]₀ - kt (First-order rate law)

3. Electrochemistry

  • E = E° - (RT/nF) ln Q (Nernst equation)
  • ΔG = -nFE (Gibbs energy and cell potential)
  • m = (Q/F)(M/n) (Faraday's law)

4. Quantum Chemistry

  • Eₙ = -13.6/n² eV (Hydrogen energy levels)
  • λ = h/mv (de Broglie wavelength)
  • Δx·Δp ≥ h/4π (Heisenberg's principle)

5. Equilibrium

  • Kc = [C]^c[D]^d / [A]^a[B]^b (Equilibrium constant)
  • Kp = Kc(RT)^Δn (Kp and Kc relation)

6. Colligative Properties

  • ΔP/P₀ = n₂/n₁ (Relative lowering of vapor pressure)
  • ΔTb = Kb·m (Boiling point elevation)
  • ΔTf = Kf·m (Freezing point depression)
  • Π = iCRT (Osmotic pressure)

7. Gaseous State

  • PV = nRT (Ideal gas law)
  • (P + a/V²)(V - b) = RT (Van der Waals equation)
  • vrms = √(3RT/M) (Root mean square velocity)

8. Solid State & Surface Chemistry

  • d = Z*M / (a³*N_A) (Density of unit cell)
  • θ = (q_ads / q_max) = (KP / 1 + KP) (Langmuir adsorption isotherm)

9. Spectroscopy & Quantum Concepts

  • E = hν (Energy of photon)
  • ν = c/λ (Wave relation)

10. Statistical Thermodynamics

  • S = k ln W (Entropy and probability)
